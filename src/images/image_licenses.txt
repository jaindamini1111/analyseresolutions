
The three facsimile images in this folder have been acquired by the National Archives of the Netherlands (http://www.gahetna.nl/), license CC-BY ( http://www.gahetna.nl/en/q-and-a/how-this-website-works/download-image ).

The images are captured from the Resolutions of the States General for the year 1740. 
They are named as: NL-HaNA_1.01.02_3795_0058.jpg, NL-HaNA_1.01.02_3795_0059.jpg and NL-HaNA_1.01.02_3795_0060.jpg.

The images are offered as examples in this release so that the code can be executed, and its processing observed in action, without extensive prior modifications.
